# Rouse Science Olympiad Website
## rousescioly.github.io
## rousescioly.org

Welcome to the **Rouse Science Olympiad Website**! This site is designed to be an all-purpose site for students, coaches, and even parents involved with Science Olympiad at Rouse. Whether you’re preparing for an upcoming competition, searching for some resources, or looking at Rouse's past achievements, this site has all of that and more!

## Features

- **Upcoming Events:** Stay up-to-date with upcoming tournaments that RSO is participating in!
- **Blog** Get insights and have a look into what the RSO team has to say! 
- **Results** Look at past results from tournaments Rouse has participated in for Science Olympiad!
- **Resources** Have a helpful set of tools at your disposal for all things Science Olympiad!

## Getting Started

To explore the site:

1. Visit the [Rouse Science Olympiad Website](https://rousescioly.github.io).
2. Navigate through the sections using navigational bar at the top of the site.
3. Explore different parts of the site and find what you need!

## Contributions

If you’re a team member, coach, or a random internet fellow who is stalking our school and want to help out:

1. Fork the repository.
2. Create a new branch for your changes.
3. Make any changes and submit a pull request.
4. If the RSO team approves, changes shall be merged.
